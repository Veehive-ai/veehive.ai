Veehive was founded by Sathish, who serves as the Founder & CEO. The leadership team includes Taj (Delivery & COO), Dixon (Product & CGO), Chakir (Head of Technology), and Fahim (Head of Engineering). The team also includes Nitesh (AI Engineer MCP).
Veehive builds four AI products: SnapaPro (AI project management), mpliphi (AI customer service for WhatsApp & Email), Studio (AI video creation with custom avatars), and HiveLink (TikTok for Business platform with calendars, events, forms, and social commerce).
Veehive has delivered AI solutions for major clients including an outsourcing company (AI job matching for Emiratisation), Phoenix Tourism One (face recognition for 80+ theme parks), Rayna Tours (agentic chatbot on WhatsApp), Costa Cruises (AI-driven cruise booking), ARKA (AI reconciliation for restaurants), and ORMAE (financial covenant monitoring with a major US bank).
Veehive specializes in AI/ML Development, Generative AI, Agentic AI, LLM & SLM Engineering, Conversational UI Platforms, Synthetic Media & AI Avatars, RAG & Vector Search, Multi-Agent Systems, MCP Architecture, Prompt Engineering, Fine-Tuning & RLHF, and Full Stack Development with MERN Stack and Framer.
Veehive has technology and strategic partnerships with NVIDIA Inception, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud, AWS, Lenovo AI Innovators, Crayon, and Presight.
Veehive is headquartered in Dubai, UAE at In5 Tech in Dubai Internet City. They operate as Veehive Tech FZ LLC with an ADGM holding company. Contact: hello@veehive.ai.
